# User Guide

## Accessing the Platform
Navigate to `http://localhost:5173` in your browser.

## Login / Register
1. **Register**: Click "Register" link. Fill in username, email, and password. If registering as an affiliate, provide affiliate details.
2. **Login**: Enter your credentials on the login page.

## Dashboard
Once logged in, you will see your dashboard.
- **Affiliates**: See a list of your submitted credit applications and their status.
- **Analysts**: See a list of all pending applications.

## Creating an Application
*Available for Affiliates*
1. Click "New Application".
2. Enter the **Amount** and **Term** (months).
3. Click "Submit".
4. The application will appear in your dashboard with `PENDING` status.

## Evaluating an Application
*Available for Analysts*
1. Locate an application in `PENDING` status.
2. Click "Evaluate".
3. The system will check the risk score and automatically Approve or Reject the application.
4. The status will update immediately.
